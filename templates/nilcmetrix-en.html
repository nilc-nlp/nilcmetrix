NILC-Metrix

Trocar para Português

NILC-Metrix gathers the metrics developed over more than a decade in NILC (Interinstitutional Center for Computational Linguistics), starting with Coh-Metrix-Port, (an adaptation of Coh-Metrix to Portuguese). The main purpose of these metrics is to assess cohesion, coherence and text complexity level. The present release makes available 200 metrics, which are described in detail here (in portuguese).
